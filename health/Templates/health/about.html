Our story

In 1993, the first Fitness Vision club opened in Delhi in the North Of India. Over the next decade and a half, Fitness Vision was present in over 16 countries, including India making it a leader in the global fitness industry. In 2018, Fitness Vision India was acquired by cure.fit, India's largest chain of fitness centres that offers energetic group workouts and multiple workout formats to choose from.
